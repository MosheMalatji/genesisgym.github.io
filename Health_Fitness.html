How Fit Are You

So are you normal weight? Overweight? Underweight? A good way to figure this out is with the Body Mass Index, or BMI. This is a way of measuring weight
based on a calculation that estimates weight as it relates to height. As noted above, there are more accurate ways to measure whether a person
is overweight, but BMI allows researchers a quick and inexpensive way to compare weights of different people easily. It also lets you compare yourself to the findings of researchers against what is considered a healthy weight.

Formulae Used To Calculate BMI

  • Heart beats per minute refers to your pulse and it is calculated using HRmax = 205.8 - (0.685 * age)

  • HRrest = heart beats per minute at rest.

  • VO2max is the maximal oxygen uptake. It is generally considered the best indicator of a person's
    cardiovascular fitness and aerobic endurance and it is calculated using, VO2max = 15 * HRmax / HRrest
