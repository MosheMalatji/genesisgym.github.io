About Genisis

Background

The first Genesis ™ Fitness Club was founded on the 16 January 2013 in Pretoria, South Africa by John Doe. In the first month
Genesis only had five members and a couple of weight machines, by the end of the year we had 30 MEMBERS and had added cardio machines.
Today Genesis Fitness is 3 years old and has 14 gyms and more than 270 members across 6 provinces in South Africa and growing.
